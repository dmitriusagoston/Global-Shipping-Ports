Global Shipping Ports

Insights

Shipping, a vital part of international commerce, is something that affects the livelihood of everyone. This visualization shows the top 100 ports in the world based off of the volume of goods transported. As the amount of goods in the port increases, so does its value in comparision to every other port. This is reflected on the map where, the greater the radius, the greater significance while, the brighter the color, the larger quantity of cargo being transported.

The volume of goods transported is measured in Twenty-Foot Equivalent Units (TEUs) which is a standarized shipping container with internal dimensions of approximately 20'x8'x8'.
